## 1. Product Overview

### What is this product?
An agentic AI driven platform that creates reports on loan applicants to improve the accuracy of the underwriting process. banks and underwriters can fill out a form with all the basic info they have about a loan applicant, and the AI will gather all info they can to provide a more accurate calculation of risk.

### Who is it for?
Banks and underwriters

### What problem does it solve?
the inefficiency and manuality of underwriting

### Core Value Proposition
This allows banks and underwriters to gather info and calculate risk faster and more accurately than if they did it alone.
